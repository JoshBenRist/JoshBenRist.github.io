Glass Cannon, Get in the Trunk, and Delta Green

January 7, 2026
TTRPG | Glass Cannon | Delta Green | Get in the Trunk

So I've been listening to Get in the Trunk ever since a friend mentioned it off hand and then pointed me at the first episode. I had never heard of the game system in use till then and instantly fell in love with the show and Delta Green. I quickly binged what there was of the show and then happily listened to it as it came out catching up to weekly releases near when they started Impossible Landscapes. I listened to their playthrough of IL and loved every minute of it. The cast had a wonderful synergy and Joe was doing a perfect job of telling the story and building an atmosphere for the players. The ending was admittedly a little shakey but this is something that people who have reviewed the campaign have pointed out before. (In particular Quinn of Quinns Quest in his review of Delta Green and IL pointed this out and even suggested rewriting it entirely to suite your players).

All of this to say: I am a fan. I have been a fan, and I will continue to be a fan... But this newest season that they've put out, Season 5, is some of the most dissapointing content I've ever listened to from the Glass Cannon crew. It all felt off in so many different ways, from story to gameplay. Things just felt weird.

I've been dissecting my thoughts on it with a friend as I listen to it week by week but I need a place to compile and properly discuss what problems I have with the show. Here is a start but I think I might spin up yet another page on this little site purely for the intent of analyzing my opinions on this seasons and understanding what went so wrong with this season.

Be on the lookout for that, but for now you have this post showing that its on my mind.
