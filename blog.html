Added the Media Page (I know it looks horrible, that comes later)

June 4, 2026
Site | Update

Alright, I still haven't bothered prettying up any of the css for the site as a whole and I haven't actually worked on this blog feature which I actually want to flesh out into a functioning system and not just static html pages BUT I have added a new page which I've been thinking about for ages and actively want to use and contribute to.

Obviously its June so clearly I've not really been giving this site the love it deserves. I want it to be something I'm always actively tinkering with and building but I find motivation to be something I lack in rather large quantities. I need some reason to actually work towards buiding this site out. This media page is one of my motivations. The goal is to be a letterboxedesque site for any media I consume and want to talk about. Books, movies, music, games. You name it. This is a feature I'm excited about and as such hopefully its something that will keep me engaged with making this place look nice.

With that all out of the way let me describe the page so that I have both a external explanation and also a record of what my thoughts are for this page in this current moment. The media page is meant to be a grid based simple page that shows the most recent thing and goes chronilogically backwards. Preferably each item would have a image, cover, album art, ect, and its title and author/director if that's relevant. Maybe room for like a quick bullet point or two, favourite track, quick couple sentence thought or something but that's it. There would hopefully be a More button that would either expand that particular tile in the page and change the css to display a more full description if I wanted to type that up or instead take you to a seperate page.

That's the plan as of right now. Its a loose image in my head that will be fleshed out, I hope, as I go along. I think I'll want to plot out some of the more concrete style elements in figma and then implement them here. We shall see. For now I wanted the barest of bones bullshit in place for that page because I had listened to some music and actually wanted to start using this feature I had in my head. Also I find its easier to style and manipulate pages and see behavior when you actually have data to manipulate so its handy to start generating that data that I can then tinker and rearrange as I see fit. Cheers.

Glass Cannon, Get in the Trunk, and Delta Green

January 7, 2026
TTRPG | Glass Cannon | Delta Green | Get in the Trunk

So I've been listening to Get in the Trunk ever since a friend mentioned it off hand and then pointed me at the first episode. I had never heard of the game system in use till then and instantly fell in love with the show and Delta Green. I quickly binged what there was of the show and then happily listened to it as it came out catching up to weekly releases near when they started Impossible Landscapes. I listened to their playthrough of IL and loved every minute of it. The cast had a wonderful synergy and Joe was doing a perfect job of telling the story and building an atmosphere for the players. The ending was admittedly a little shakey but this is something that people who have reviewed the campaign have pointed out before. (In particular Quinn of Quinns Quest in his review of Delta Green and IL pointed this out and even suggested rewriting it entirely to suite your players).

All of this to say: I am a fan. I have been a fan, and I will continue to be a fan... But this newest season that they've put out, Season 5, is some of the most dissapointing content I've ever listened to from the Glass Cannon crew. It all felt off in so many different ways, from story to gameplay. Things just felt weird.

I've been dissecting my thoughts on it with a friend as I listen to it week by week but I need a place to compile and properly discuss what problems I have with the show. Here is a start but I think I might spin up yet another page on this little site purely for the intent of analyzing my opinions on this seasons and understanding what went so wrong with this season.

Be on the lookout for that, but for now you have this post showing that its on my mind.
